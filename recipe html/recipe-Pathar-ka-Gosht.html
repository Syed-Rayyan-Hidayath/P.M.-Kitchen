Pathar ka Gosht

← Back to Menu

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton (thinly sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon raw papaya paste (for tenderizing)
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Charcoal stone or large griddle
  • Lemon wedges and sliced onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix mutton with ginger-garlic paste, papaya paste, red chili powder, turmeric, garam masala, coriander powder, and salt. Let marinate for 2-3 hours.
  2. Heat a thick stone slab (or heavy griddle) over medium-high heat until very hot.
  3. Grease the stone with a little oil or ghee. Place marinated mutton slices directly on the hot stone.
  4. Cook each side for 5-7 minutes, pressing down occasionally, until the meat is tender and slightly charred.
  5. Once cooked, remove from the stone and garnish with lemon wedges and sliced onions.
  6. Serve hot as an appetizer or side with naan or parathas.

Extras

Pathar ka Gosht is a traditional Hyderabadi dish, known for its unique cooking method using a hot stone. This imparts a smoky flavor, which enhances the taste of the marinated meat.
