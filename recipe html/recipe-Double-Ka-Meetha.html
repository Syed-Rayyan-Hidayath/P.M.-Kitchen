Double Ka Meetha

← Back to Menu

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of white bread
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • 1/4 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 2 tbsp chopped almonds and pistachios
  • 2 tbsp raisins
  • Edible silver leaf (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cut the bread slices into halves or quarters and toast them in a pan with ghee until golden brown on both sides. Set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, mix sugar and water to make a sugar syrup. Bring it to a boil and simmer until slightly thickened.
  3. In another saucepan, heat the milk and condensed milk together. Add cardamom powder and simmer for a few minutes.
  4. Arrange the fried bread pieces in a baking dish, then pour the sugar syrup over them evenly.
  5. Pour the milk mixture over the bread, ensuring the pieces are well soaked.
  6. Sprinkle chopped nuts and raisins on top.
  7. Let it sit for 10 minutes to absorb, then garnish with edible silver leaf if desired.
  8. Serve warm or chilled.

Extras

For a richer taste, use full-fat milk, and garnish with extra nuts. Double Ka Meetha tastes even better when it has rested and absorbed all the flavors.
