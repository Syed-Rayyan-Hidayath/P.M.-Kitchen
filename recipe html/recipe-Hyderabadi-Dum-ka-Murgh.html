Hyderabadi Dum ka Murgh

← Back to Menu

Ingredients

  • 1 kg chicken pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1 cup fried onions, crushed
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1/4 cup ghee or clarified butter
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon garam masala
  • 1/2 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves, chopped
  • A few saffron strands (optional, soaked in warm milk)
  • Fried onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, coriander powder, cumin powder, and salt. Add chicken pieces and coat well. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour (preferably overnight).
  2. In a heavy-bottomed pot, add ghee and pour in the marinated chicken. Add crushed fried onions, cream, chopped coriander, mint leaves, and saffron milk. Mix everything gently to coat the chicken.
  3. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid, sealing it with dough if possible. Cook on a low flame for 45-60 minutes, until the chicken is tender and flavors are well absorbed.
  4. Garnish with fried onions and fresh coriander. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Extras

For a richer taste, you can add cashew paste to the marinade. Adjust the spice level according to your preference.
