Khubani ka Murabba

← Back to Menu

Ingredients

  • 500g dried apricots (khubani)
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Almonds for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Rinse the dried apricots thoroughly and soak them in water overnight or for at least 6-8 hours.
  2. After soaking, remove the apricots from the water (reserving the water) and de-seed them. Crack open the seeds to remove the kernels if you wish to add them to the murabba.
  3. In a large pot, add the soaked apricots, reserved water, and sugar. Cook on medium heat, stirring occasionally, until the sugar dissolves completely.
  4. Add cardamom powder and continue to cook until the syrup thickens slightly and the apricots become soft and glazed, about 20-30 minutes.
  5. Stir in lemon juice and cook for another 5 minutes to enhance the flavor and preserve the murabba.
  6. Remove from heat and let it cool completely. Garnish with almond slivers or the apricot kernels if desired.
  7. Transfer to a sterilized jar and store in the refrigerator. Khubani ka Murabba can be enjoyed as a dessert or spread on toast.

Extras

Khubani ka Murabba is a sweet preserve popular in Hyderabadi cuisine. You can adjust the sweetness by adding more or less sugar, depending on your preference.
