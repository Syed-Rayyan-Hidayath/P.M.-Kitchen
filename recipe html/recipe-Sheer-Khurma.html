Sheer Khurma

← Back to Menu

Ingredients

  • 1 liter milk
  • 1/2 cup vermicelli (seviyan)
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 1/4 cup chopped dates
  • 1/4 cup mixed nuts (almonds, pistachios, cashews), chopped
  • 1 tablespoon raisins
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 2-3 saffron strands (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and lightly roast the vermicelli until golden brown. Set aside.
  2. In the same pan, sauté the chopped dates, nuts, and raisins until fragrant.
  3. In a separate pot, bring the milk to a boil and let it simmer until slightly thickened.
  4. Add the roasted vermicelli to the milk and cook until soft.
  5. Stir in sugar, cardamom powder, saffron strands (if using), and the sautéed nuts and dates. Mix well.
  6. Simmer for a few more minutes until the flavors meld, then remove from heat.
  7. Serve warm or chilled, garnished with additional nuts if desired.

Extras

Sheer Khurma is a festive dessert enjoyed during Eid celebrations. Its rich, creamy texture and the mix of nuts make it a comforting and indulgent treat.
