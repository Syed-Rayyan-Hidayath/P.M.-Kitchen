Qubani ka Meetha

← Back to Menu

Ingredients

  • 250g dried apricots (qubani)
  • 1/2 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Water as needed
  • Whipped cream or vanilla ice cream for serving
  • Chopped almonds or pistachios for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the dried apricots and soak them in water overnight.
  2. Drain the apricots, reserving the soaking water. Remove the seeds, if present.
  3. In a saucepan, add the soaked apricots and enough reserved water to cover them.
  4. Cook over medium heat until the apricots soften and start to break down.
  5. Add sugar and continue to cook until the mixture thickens to a jam-like consistency.
  6. Let it cool slightly, then serve warm or at room temperature, topped with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream.
  7. Garnish with chopped almonds or pistachios for added texture and flavor.
