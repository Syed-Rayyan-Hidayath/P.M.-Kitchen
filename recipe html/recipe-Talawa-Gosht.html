Talawa Gosht

← Back to Menu

Ingredients

  • 500g mutton, cut into thin slices or small pieces
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2-3 tablespoons oil (for frying)
  • Sliced onions and fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, marinate the mutton with ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, salt, and lemon juice. Let it sit for 1-2 hours.
  2. Heat oil in a pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the marinated mutton to the pan and cook, stirring occasionally, until the meat is browned and cooked through. Add a little water if needed to avoid sticking.
  4. Continue to cook until the water evaporates, and the mutton is crispy and well-browned.
  5. Once done, remove from heat and garnish with sliced onions and fresh coriander.
  6. Serve hot as an appetizer or with naan or rice.

Extras

Talawa Gosht is a popular Hyderabadi fried mutton dish, known for its simplicity and robust flavors. It pairs well with parathas, naan, or rice.
