Badam ki Jali

← Back to Menu

Ingredients

  • 1 cup almond powder
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • A pinch of saffron (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • Edible silver leaf (optional, for decoration)

Instructions

  1. In a saucepan, mix sugar and water, then heat until the sugar dissolves completely.
  2. Add saffron and cardamom powder to the sugar syrup, stirring well.
  3. Slowly add almond powder to the mixture, stirring constantly to form a smooth, pliable dough.
  4. Allow the dough to cool slightly, then roll it out thinly on a flat surface.
  5. Use a cookie cutter to cut out intricate designs (like floral or circular shapes) to create the "jali" (net-like) effect.
  6. Place the shaped pieces on a tray and apply edible silver leaf for an elegant finish, if desired.
  7. Let the Badam ki Jali pieces set for a few hours, then serve or store in an airtight container.

Extras

For a finer texture, you can use blanched almonds to make the almond powder at home. Adjust sugar to taste for a more or less sweet version.
