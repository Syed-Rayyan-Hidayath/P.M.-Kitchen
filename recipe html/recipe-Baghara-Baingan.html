Baghara Baingan

← Back to Menu

Ingredients

  • 8-10 small eggplants (baingan)
  • 1/4 cup peanuts
  • 1/4 cup sesame seeds
  • 2 tbsp grated coconut
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 tbsp tamarind pulp
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a pan, dry roast peanuts, sesame seeds, and grated coconut until golden brown. Let cool, then grind into a fine paste with a little water.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan and add cumin and mustard seeds until they splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add the ground paste, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt. Sauté the mixture for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Wash and slit the eggplants halfway, keeping them whole. Add them to the pan and coat with the masala mixture.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat, stirring occasionally, until the eggplants are tender.
  7. Add tamarind pulp and simmer for another 5 minutes until the flavors meld.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.

Extras

For extra flavor, cook on low heat and allow the spices to penetrate the eggplants fully. Adjust the amount of tamarind pulp based on your desired tanginess.
