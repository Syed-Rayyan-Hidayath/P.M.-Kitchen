Hyderabadi Biryani

← Back to Menu

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 500g marinated meat (chicken or mutton)
  • 2 large onions, sliced
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Spices: bay leaves, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon
  • Saffron strands soaked in warm milk
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then cook until half-done.
  2. In a large pot, layer the marinated meat, followed by a layer of par-cooked rice.
  3. Top with fried onions, saffron milk, and a few dollops of ghee.
  4. Cover the pot tightly and cook on low heat for 20-30 minutes until the rice and meat are fully cooked.
  5. Gently mix and serve hot with raita or salad.

Extras

To enhance the flavor, cook on a "dum" (sealed) setup where the steam remains inside the pot for an authentic taste.
