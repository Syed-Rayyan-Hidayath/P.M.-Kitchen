Haleem

← Back to Menu

Ingredients

  • 1 cup wheat grains
  • 1/2 cup lentils (split chickpeas, red lentils, and yellow lentils)
  • 500g boneless mutton or chicken
  • 2 large onions, sliced and fried
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • Spices: bay leaves, cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, cumin seeds
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Chopped fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • Ghee (clarified butter)
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak the wheat and lentils separately overnight.
  2. Cook the soaked wheat until tender, and blend it into a paste.
  3. In a large pot, cook the mutton or chicken with ginger-garlic paste, yogurt, and spices until tender.
  4. Add the blended wheat and cooked lentils to the meat mixture, stirring well.
  5. Simmer on low heat, stirring regularly to avoid sticking, until everything blends into a thick paste.
  6. Garnish with fried onions, fresh coriander, and mint leaves. Serve hot with naan or bread.

Extras

For an authentic taste, cook the Haleem on low heat for several hours, stirring occasionally for a smooth, creamy consistency.
